Main Services of Liwach Books

Our services range from Book Swapping and buying to delivery to your desired address at a low cost. We are thriving to make it easy for you to get a book.

Swap Easily

We make it really easy for you to get a book you have your eyes on just at a cost of a book you're willing to give out and a birr (1 ETB).

Borrow easily

Borrow high-priced, high-demand or any book for that matter for a reasonable time limit at a reasonable price.

Of course, BUY BOOKS as well

Books are worth the dime. When you are ready to invest on a book, we provide a hassele-free service, making it easy for you to get the book right in your hands.
