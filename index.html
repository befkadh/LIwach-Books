About Us

We are Liwach

We are an Addis Ababa-based company focused on swapping materials to make them abundantly available to everyone in Ethiopia, That's the main goal. Our kick=off being focused on books, we want to express how much we want to fill the gap between readers and books. We see that paperback culture is being less of a reality and pirating has been the new way of supplying oneself. We see that there is a very unreal price tag, especially on English books and unavailablity as well of some books of that kind. So understandibly, people have chosen easy digitally pirated books. But we want to change that as it hurts authors and discourage creativity. We want to help Ethiopians get any book with a price tag of 1 birr when they donate their already-read books.
